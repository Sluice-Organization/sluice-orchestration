# BayFlow - Strategy

## Market
- **Vertical:** Auto repair shop management
- **Target persona:** Independent shop owners with 1-15 techs, currently using paper or outdated desktop software
- **TAM estimate:** 250,000+ independent auto repair shops in the US
- **Key competitors:** Shop-Ware ($199-399/mo), Tekmetric ($199-399/mo), Mitchell 1 (legacy), ShopBoss ($79-199/mo)
- **Gap:** Competitors are expensive, clunky, and over-featured. No one owns the "simple and beautiful" position for small shops.

## Product
- **Core features:** Digital inspections with photos, estimate builder, one-click invoice, kanban shop board, customer texting, VIN auto-decode, vehicle history, job profitability tracking
- **PLG mechanics:** Free 14-day trial, self-serve signup, instant value (create first repair order in under 2 minutes)
- **Data lock-in:** Customer records, vehicle history, inspection photos, financial history. Switching cost increases with every repair order.

## Pricing
- **Solo:** $79/mo (1 tech, 50 vehicles/month)
- **Pro:** $129/mo (5 techs, unlimited vehicles, texting, profitability tracking)
- **Shop:** $179/mo (15 techs, scheduling, analytics, API)
- **Free trial:** 14 days, no credit card

## Distribution
- **Primary channel:** SEO (shop owners Google their problems)
- **Secondary channel:** Short-form video/reels (show the product in action)
- **Tertiary:** Auto repair forums, Facebook groups, directory listings

## Validation
- **Revenue Ceiling Test:** PASS - 1,000 shops at $129/mo avg = $129K MRR
- **Distribution Test:** PASS - High-intent keywords ("auto repair shop software", "digital vehicle inspection app"), strong reel potential (before/after shop workflows)
- **Exit validation:** Valsoft has acquired multiple automotive software companies. Proven acquirer interest.
