# Market Research - Validated Vertical SaaS Niches

## Research Date: 2026-03-04

## Key Insight: What Valsoft Buys (119 acquisitions, $150M+ war chest)

Valsoft is the most active vertical SaaS acquirer. Their pattern:
- Mission-critical software in boring industries
- Companies often 10-30+ years old with sticky customers
- Verticals: automotive, dental, construction, workforce management, transportation/logistics, energy, broadcast/media, hospitality, car rental, manufacturing, security/public safety, education, animal health

## Acquire.com Validated Demand (real businesses that sold)

- Shopify booking app: $1.02M revenue, sold for $3.8M (99% retention, 2,100 customers)
- Real estate SaaS suite: $842K revenue, sold for $1.7M (78% YoY growth)
- Website builder for creatives: $600K revenue, sold for $1.6M (4,300 customers, <2% churn)
- AI resume tool: $1.5M revenue, sold for $3M (1,000+ customers)
- Gamified membership SaaS: $140K revenue, sold for $490K

## Key Patterns from Valsoft Portfolio
1. They buy software in industries where switching costs are enormous
2. Most acquired companies are 15-30+ years old (proves stickiness)
3. Verticals where the software becomes the system of record
4. Industries with compliance requirements (dental, energy, security)
5. B2B verticals where the buyer is a business, not a consumer
